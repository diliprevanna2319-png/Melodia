# 🎶 Melodia — Free Music, Every Genre

Listen to all kinds of music, completely free and 100% legal — no account, no subscription, no ads.

## What you can play
- **📻 Internet Radio** — 30,000+ live stations covering every genre & language (Bollywood, Tamil, pop, rock, lo-fi, classical, EDM, jazz, devotional, and more), powered by the free [radio-browser.info](https://www.radio-browser.info) directory.
- **📚 Free Library** — millions of free & public-domain songs, full albums and live concerts from the [Internet Archive](https://archive.org).
- **💾 My Files** — play music files you already own; they stay on your device, nothing is uploaded.
- **❤️ Favorites** — save stations and tracks you love (stored locally in your browser).

## Run it
Just open **`index.html`** in any modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox). That's it — no install, no setup.

It's an installable, offline-capable **PWA**: in Chrome/Edge click the install icon in the address bar to add it to your desktop or phone home screen. (The app shell works offline; streaming naturally needs internet.)

## A note on "any song"
Streaming *every* commercial/copyrighted song for free isn't legal (that's what paid services like Spotify license). Melodia focuses on the enormous amount of music that **is** free and legal to stream — which still covers virtually every genre and language.

## Tech
Plain HTML + CSS + vanilla JavaScript. No framework, no build step, no backend, no API keys.
- `index.html` — app shell & player bar
- `styles.css` — styles
- `js/sources.js` — radio + Internet Archive data sources
- `js/app.js` — UI, navigation, audio player
- `manifest.json`, `sw.js`, `icon.svg` — PWA bits
